Hull Foodshare

Hello!

Welcome to Hull Foodshare

We are a group of students from the Universty of Hull wanting to help stop food waste by creating a website to help people share their unused food products.

Our aim is to encourage people to engage in the fight against food waste! Food is essential to all of us... let's not let it go to waste!

We accept every kind of food from all parts of the world accepting each other differences and acknowledging that everyfood is unique. We encourage people to share their food with us either through our social medaia platform or through our website with alongside the type of food you would like to share.

Get Involved

How can you help?

We are open to the public and to students looking for ways to help the community.

You can volunteer your time to help with the food share, with a few hours once a week or fortnight. This will generally entail assisting anyone who would like to drop off or make collections of food.
